About the College

Main Gate — PDACEK Campus, Kalaburagi

Poojya Doddappa Appa College of Engineering (PDACEK) was founded in 1958 by the HKE Society — the first institution of the society. Affiliated to VTU, the campus spans 71 acres with departments in Civil, Mechanical-Automobile, ECE, EEE, CSE, ISE, EIE, CCT, IPE, Basic Sciences, and more.
